🌱 New to DeFi? Learn the basics

▼ Show
💰
What is APY?
Annual Percentage Yield — the return you earn per year. 10% APY on $1,000 means ~$100 earned in a year.
🏊
What is a Liquidity Pool?
You deposit tokens into a shared pool. Traders use the pool to swap tokens, and you earn a share of their fees.
🏦
What is Lending?
You lend your crypto to borrowers through a smart contract. They pay interest, you earn yield.
⚠️
What are the risks?
Smart contract bugs, market volatility, and impermanent loss. Never invest more than you can afford to lose.
📊
What is TVL?
Total Value Locked — how much money is in the pool. Higher TVL generally means the protocol is more trusted.
🔗
What is a blockchain?
A decentralized network where transactions happen without banks. Ethereum, SUI, Solana are all different blockchains.
